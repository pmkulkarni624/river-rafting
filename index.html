Before You Paddle

Essential tips for a safe and epic trip

🦺

Always Wear a PFD

A properly fitted personal flotation device is non-negotiable on any river, regardless of difficulty.

🪖

Helmet Up

Protect your head from rocks and paddle impacts. Helmets are mandatory on Class III and above.

🌡️

Know the Water Temp

Cold water can cause shock and hypothermia fast. Dress for immersion, not just the air temperature.

📋

Scout Unfamiliar Rapids

Always stop and walk the bank to inspect any rapid you haven't run before. When in doubt, portage.

👥

Never Raft Alone

Always go with a group or hire a licensed guide. Accidents happen — you need people who can help.

🗺️

Check Water Levels

River conditions change with rainfall and seasons. Always check gauge readings before your trip.
